What is Astrare ?

 Astrare is a desktop program that helps amateur astronomers that provide utilities to compute ephemerids, events, generating skycharts and more, in order to prepare their observation sessions.
 It's compatible with Windows and Linux, and will support MacOS and Android in the future.

Document generating

 Astrare provides many tools for generating ready-to-print PDF documents to have all what you need for your observations without bringing your computer. It also support .txt files.

  • Ephemeris summary
  • Skycharts
  • Events list (for month or year)
  • yearly graphic almanachs
